Coffee - Cocktails - Community

Honeycomb is a cafe and cocktail bar in one. Enjoy our decadent baked goods and speciality coffee during the day, you will be enticed to return for a cocktail and small bites in the evening. Located in the middle of the ever buzzing beehive street, this unique space is a true oasis and a must-visit for any coffee and cocktail lover!

Introducing Cocktails!!

You asked and we listened! We have had a lot of feedback suggesting longer opening hours and an evening vibe. We are proud to introduce specialist cocktails to our menu which can be ordered after 6pm or anytime for Sunday brunch!
Whethere you're craving an espresson martini or a purple rain, come on down to Honeycomb.

Venue for hire

Do you have an event you want to host but not sure where to host it? We host small gatherings, book clubs, birthday celebrations, games nights, karaoke, drag shows, workshops, you name it! Choose between the vibrant atmosphere of the cafe or the more intimate setting of the garden. As part of our plan to give back to the community, local charities can book our venue for free. Contact us for booking enquiries.
